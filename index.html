Brief Biography: Zhijun Yang is a Ph.D. student in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) advised by Prof. Yu Hua. He received B.E. degree in Computer Science from HUST in 2023. His research interests include distributed computing, emerging memory and network technologies, and resource pooling.
